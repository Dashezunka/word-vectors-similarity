Claude Hopkins’ book, Scientific Advertising, is one of the most influential copywriting books of all time.

In fact, it’s so good that David Ogilvy once said:

“Nobody should be allowed to have anything to do with advertising until he has read this book seven times. It changed the course of my life.”

But what makes this book so powerful? And most importantly, are these principles still relevant today?

The answer is a resounding yes.

Scientific Advertising is a holy grail for advertising and most of its teachings are still highly applicable in 2020.
